This Privacy Policy describes our policies and procedures on the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use the services provided by KK Mega777. This Privacy Policy applies to all users of our mobile application and services.

Limited data collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal or non-personal data from users.

The app:

  • Does not request any personal information
  • However, we use third-party services (Google AdMob and Firebase Crashlytics) which may collect limited device and usage data as part of their functionality

Advertising and AdMob

Our app displays rewarded ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain device and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, such as:

  • Device information (e.g., OS version, device model)
  • Advertising ID (used for ad personalization)
  • App usage data (e.g., how often ads are watched)

To learn more about how Google uses this information, please visit Google's Advertising Policy.

Crash Reporting with Firebase Crashlytics

We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymized crash reports in order to improve app stability and performance. Information collected may include:

  • Device information (e.g., OS version, device state)
  • Error logs (e.g., crash stack trace)
  • App version and usage context

No personally identifiable information is intentionally collected via Crashlytics.

For more information, please refer to Firebase's Privacy and Security documentation.
